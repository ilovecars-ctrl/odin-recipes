The Best Churro Recipe!

Description

The churro is a simple yet classic dessert throughout Latin America, especially in Spain. Please enjoy this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg White

    • Steps

    1. In saucepan add 1/2 cup milk, 1/2 cup water and pinch of salt. Put over medium heat until boil.
    2. Add 1 cup flour into small bowl, add into the milk. Cook in saucepan for 1 1/2 minutes over medium heat. Stir often.
    3. Put the dough in a bowl. Add one egg white. Knead until dough doesn't stick to your hands. Let it rest for 10 minutes. Be careful not to burn your hands!
    4. Pour olive oil into a frying pan and put it over medium heat until it reaches 356 F.
    5. Load dough into churro maker. Pipe lines about 4" long.
    6. Fry churros for 3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Place on a paper towel to absorb oil.
    7. Coat churros with sugar and enjoy!
