The Best Baklava Recipe!

Description

This is a delicious easy to make recipe for a classic Middle Eastern Dessert! Famous for its sweet flavor and flaky texture, this recipe is sure to impress all your guests! No one knows the true origins of Baklava but it is widely speculated that originated in Greece.

Ingredients

  • Phyllo Dough
  • Chopped Walnuts
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Saigon Cinnamon
  • Syrup of Water, Sugar, and Vanilla

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Butter all sides of the 9x13 pan
  2. Chop the nuts and toss them with cinnamon in a bowl. Set the bowl aside.
  3. Unroll phyllo dough. Cut stack in half. Cover dough with dampened cloth. Place two sheets of dough in pan. Butter with a brush. Repeat until you have 8 sheets layered.
  4. Sprinkle nut mixture on top. Top with two sheets of dough.
  5. All the way to the bottom of the pan, cut diamond or square shapes. Bake for 50 minutes until it is gold and crisp.
  6. While the Baklava is melting boil sugar and water together until the sugar is melted. Add vanilla and honey.Simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove Baklava from the oven and immediatley spoon sauce over it.
  8. Serve in cupcake papers and enjoy!
