The best Turkish Delight Recipe!

Description

Turkish Delight, also known as Lokum, is a soft squishy cube, typically filled with pistachios on the inside and coated with powdered sugar. It is generally thought to be created in 18th century Turkey and Iran.

Ingredients

  • Rosewater
  • Water
  • Lemon Juice
  • Corn Starch
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Orange Extract
  • Raspberry Extract
  • Salt
  • Pistachios
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Red food coloring

Steps

  1. Line baking dish with parchment paper
  2. Combine powdered sugar and cornstarch to dust over the candy.
  3. Combine sugar, lemon juice, and 1 Â¾ cups water into saucepan and set over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.
  4. In small bowl combine 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons cornstarch with 1/2 cup cold water. Whisk into hot sugar syrup. Whisk to break up any lumps. Bring to a boil. Simmer for 20 minutes. Whisk often.
  5. Add 2 drops of red food coloring. Stir in the rosewater. Stir in chopped pistachios. Pour into dish. Cool overnight.
  6. Cut into pieces and coat with powdered sugar and decorate with edible glitter.
  7. Enjoy!
